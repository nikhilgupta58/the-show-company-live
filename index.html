Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We specialize in college shows, corporate events, and large-scale auditorium performances. Our services include standup comedy shows, EDM DJ nights, singing concerts, and live band performances. Whether it's a campus fest, corporate gala, or public auditorium event, we handle end-to-end production.

You can reach us through the Customer Query form on this website or email us directly. For college shows, we recommend booking 2-3 months in advance. For corporate events, we can accommodate shorter timelines depending on availability. Our team will work with you to understand your requirements and create a customized proposal.

We're always looking for talented artists! Send your portfolio, showreel, and a brief introduction to talent@theshowcompany.com. Our talent acquisition team reviews all submissions and reaches out to artists whose style aligns with our upcoming projects. We offer management, booking, and production support.

We're based in India and operate pan-India. We've executed shows in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, and numerous other cities. For college shows, we've worked with institutions like BITS Pilani, Graphic Era, and many more. We can also arrange international events on request.

Our end-to-end production includes artist booking, stage design, sound & lighting (through our partner VIGOR MENTORS), venue coordination, technical setup, event management, and post-event support. We handle everything from concept to execution so you can focus on enjoying the show.

For major events with headlining artists, we recommend 3-6 months in advance. College fests and corporate events typically require 1-3 months. For smaller shows or last-minute requirements, reach out and we'll check availability. Early booking ensures better artist options and venue availability.
