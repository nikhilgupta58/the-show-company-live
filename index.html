Who We Are

Great laughter brings
people together.

At The Show Company, we believe great laughter and live entertainment bring people together like nothing else. Specialising in stand-up comedy, we curate and deliver unforgettable comedy shows across the country, featuring both emerging talent and established performers.

Beyond comedy, we design vibrant live experiences at colleges, universities, and cultural venues — from energetic music gigs and EDM nights to engaging campus festivals and large-scale auditorium productions. Our team manages every detail, from artist booking and stage design to sound, lighting, and post-event support, working closely with our partners to ensure a flawless experience every time.

Our mission is simple: to create high-quality, memorable entertainment that connects audiences, celebrates creativity, and leaves every crowd smiling long after the curtains close. We are also expanding into advertising and brand collaborations, helping companies connect with audiences through the power of live entertainment.

Frequently Asked Questions

We specialize in college shows, corporate events, and large-scale auditorium performances. Our services include standup comedy shows, EDM DJ nights, singing concerts, and live band performances. Whether it's a campus fest, corporate gala, or public auditorium event, we handle end-to-end production including artist booking, stage design, sound and lighting (through our partner VIGOR MENTORS), venue coordination, and post-event support.

You can reach us through the enquiry form on this website, email us at talent@theshowcompany.com, or call us directly. For college shows, we recommend booking 2–3 months in advance. For corporate events, we can accommodate shorter timelines depending on availability. Our team will work with you to understand your requirements and create a customized proposal that fits your budget and vision.

We're always looking for talented artists! Send your portfolio, showreel, and a brief introduction to talent@theshowcompany.com. Our talent acquisition team reviews all submissions and reaches out to artists whose style aligns with our upcoming projects. We offer management, booking, and full production support to help artists focus on their craft while we handle the logistics.

We are based in India and operate pan-India. We've executed shows in Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Dehradun, Jaipur, and numerous other cities. For college shows, we've worked with institutions like Graphic Era, DPS, and many more. We can also arrange international events on request — reach out and we'll discuss the scope with you.

Our end-to-end production includes artist booking and management, stage design and setup, world-class sound and lighting (through our partner VIGOR MENTORS), venue sourcing and coordination, technical crew, event management on the day, promotion support, and post-event wrap-up. We handle everything from concept to curtain call so you can focus entirely on enjoying the show.

For major events with headlining artists, we recommend 3–6 months in advance to secure the best talent and venues. College fests typically require 1–3 months of lead time, and corporate events can sometimes be accommodated in as little as 2–4 weeks depending on complexity. The earlier you reach out, the better the artist selection and venue availability. Don't hesitate to contact us even for last-minute requirements — we'll do our best!
